Every ebook follows this 8-step questline structure:

```
1. Start Journey (Read 10 mins) ‚Üí +10 XP
2. Checkpoint (Answer 1 question) ‚Üí +25 XP  
3. Apply Learning (Micro-action) ‚Üí +25 XP
4. Reflect Deeply (Journal prompt) ‚Üí +15 XP
5. Continue Reading (Read 10 mins) ‚Üí +10 XP
6. Chapter Complete (Manual mark) ‚Üí +50 XP
7. Finish Book (All chapters) ‚Üí +100 XP
8. Integration Week (Optional) ‚Üí +50 XP
```

## üìñ Individual Book Content

### "Healing From Within" 
**Category**: Self-Worth Series  
**Quest Focus**: Building unshakeable self-worth

#### Chapter Breakdown (8 chapters)
1. **Understanding Your Worth** - Foundation setting
2. **Releasing Past Wounds** - Healing work  
3. **Self-Compassion Practice** - Daily tools
4. **Inner Critic Transformation** - Mindset shifts
5. **Boundaries with Self** - Self-respect
6. **Celebrating Progress** - Recognition
7. **Future Self Visioning** - Goal setting
8. **Integration & Maintenance** - Sustainability

#### Sample Quest Steps
- **Read Quest**: "Read Chapter 1: Understanding Your Worth (10 mins)"
- **Checkpoint**: "On a scale of 1-10, how would you rate your current self-worth? What influences this rating?"
- **Apply Quest**: "Write down 3 things you appreciate about yourself today"
- **Reflect Quest**: "What would change in your life if you truly believed you were worthy of love?"

#### Reflection Prompts
- "What does self-worth mean to you right now?"
- "Describe a moment when you felt truly at peace with yourself"
- "What's one limiting belief you're ready to release?"
- "How has your relationship with yourself changed recently?"
- "What would you tell your younger self about self-love?"

### "Boundaries in Love"
**Category**: Relationships Series  
**Quest Focus**: Healthy relationship boundaries

#### Chapter Breakdown (6 chapters)
1. **What Are Boundaries?** - Definition & importance
2. **Boundary Myths** - Common misconceptions
3. **Identifying Your Needs** - Self-awareness
4. **Communication Scripts** - Practical tools
5. **Maintaining Boundaries** - Consistency
6. **Boundaries as Love** - Reframing perspective

#### Sample Quest Steps
- **Read Quest**: "Read Chapter 2: Boundary Myths (10 mins)"
- **Checkpoint**: "Which boundary myth have you believed? How has it affected your relationships?"
- **Apply Quest**: "Practice saying 'I need to think about that' instead of immediate 'yes'"
- **Reflect Quest**: "Where in your life do you need stronger boundaries?"

#### Reflection Prompts
- "Where in your life do you need stronger boundaries?"
- "What makes it difficult for you to say 'no'?"
- "Describe a healthy boundary you've successfully set"
- "How do you feel when your boundaries are respected?"
- "What's one boundary you want to practice this week?"
